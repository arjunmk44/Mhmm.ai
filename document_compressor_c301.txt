COMPRESSOR C-301 MAINTENANCE PROCEDURE AND SPECIFICATION
Equipment Tag: C-301
Type: Reciprocating Gas Compressor
Associated Vessel: V-204
Associated Sensor: PT-301, TT-301
Location: Unit 400 Gas Treatment Facility

Operational Parameters:
Suction Pressure: 5.2 bar
Discharge Pressure: 42.0 bar
Operating Temperature: 82 C
Max Allowed Temperature: 110 C

Maintenance Schedule:
Inspect suction valves every 2000 operating hours.
Check crankcase oil level daily.
If TT-301 exceeds 100 C, escalate alert to high severity and initiate emergency shutdown procedure SOP-301.
